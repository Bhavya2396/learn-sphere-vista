🔬 Pure Substances

A pure substance consists of only one type of particle with the same chemical nature throughout.

  • Fixed composition
  • Definite properties
  • Cannot be separated by physical methods

🌊 Homogeneous Mixtures

Solutions have uniform composition throughout. Particles are evenly distributed.

  • Particles not visible
  • No light scattering
  • Stable mixture

🏔️ Heterogeneous Mixtures

Non-uniform composition with physically distinct parts visible.

  • Particles visible
  • Can be filtered
  • Settles over time
