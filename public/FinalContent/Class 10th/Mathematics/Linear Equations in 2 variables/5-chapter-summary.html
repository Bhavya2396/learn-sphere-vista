Three Solution Methods

📊
Graphical
Plot lines to find intersection. Best for visualization.
🔄
Substitution
Express one variable, substitute. Best for simple equations.
➖
Elimination
Make coefficients equal, eliminate. Best for complex problems.

All three methods solve the same problems - choose based on convenience!

Ratio Test for Solution Types
