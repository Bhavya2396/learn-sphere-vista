Common Hindi Prefixes (उपसर्ग)

Prefixes are added before the root word to modify its meaning. Here are some common Hindi prefixes:

Prefix Meaning Example
अ Negation, absence अशुद्ध (impure) - शुद्ध (pure)
अन Negation, absence अनपढ़ (illiterate) - पढ़ (read)
सु Good, well सुगंध (fragrance) - गंध (smell)
दुर् Bad, difficult दुर्गंध (bad smell) - गंध (smell)
प्र Forward, forth प्रगति (progress) - गति (movement)
अज्ञान
Ignorance (अ + ज्ञान)
सुपुत्र
Good son (सु + पुत्र)
दुर्भाग्य
Misfortune (दुर् + भाग्य)

Common Hindi Suffixes (प्रत्यय)

Suffixes are added after the root word to modify its meaning. Here are some common Hindi suffixes:

Suffix Usage Example
वाला Denotes occupation or possession दूधवाला (milkman) - दूध (milk)
ई Forms abstract nouns मिठाई (sweetness) - मीठा (sweet)
ता Forms abstract nouns सुंदरता (beauty) - सुंदर (beautiful)
आई Forms abstract nouns बड़ाई (greatness) - बड़ा (big)
आना Forms verbs सुखाना (to dry) - सूखा (dry)
सच्चाई
Truth (सच + आई)
मधुरता
Sweetness (मधुर + ता)
फूलवाला
Florist (फूल + वाला)
